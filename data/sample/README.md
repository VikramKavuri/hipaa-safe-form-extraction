# Sample data

This folder holds **synthetic, public-safe** fire-drill forms used by the demo,
the smoke tests, and the evaluation harness.

**No real forms / PHI are ever placed here or committed** (`.gitignore` enforces
this for the `data/private/` and `data/raw/` folders).

Synthetic sample forms + their hand-labeled ground truth are generated in
**Phase 3** (see [`docs/ROADMAP.md`](../../docs/ROADMAP.md)) and will be checked in
here alongside `eval/ground_truth/`.
